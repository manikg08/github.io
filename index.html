My Mission

As a master's student in Computational Engineering at Friedrich-Alexander University, I'm on a mission to revolutionize how we approach engineering simulations. My work focuses on integrating cutting-edge AI techniques with traditional physics-based models to create more accurate, efficient, and intelligent engineering solutions.

Currently working on my thesis at Schaeffler Group, I'm developing advanced models for thermo-mechanical fatigue analysis of composite materials. This work combines finite element methods with machine learning to predict material behavior under complex loading conditions.
